Facts about Flowers

A flower is a reproductive organ found in most flowering plants. The flower structure contains the plant's genitals, and its function is to produce seeds through sexed propagation. In many smaller plants, the flower is by far the most conspicuous part. Therefore, such plants in their entirety are often simply called "flowers". In certain plants, the flowers give rise to the fruits during the season." Source: Wikipedia
